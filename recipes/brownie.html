Fudgy Brownie

Who doesn't love a good fudgy brownie on a lazy day? Recipe by Savour Easy.

Ingredients

  • Butter 1/2 cup
  • Granulated sugar 1 cup
  • Eggs 1 full and 1 yolk
  • Vanilla extract 1 tsp
  • Flour 1/2 cup
  • Cocoa powder 1/2 cup
  • Salt 1/2 tsp
  • Semi-sweet chocolate chips 1 cup

Instructions

  1. Mix melted butter and sugar in a bowl.
  2. Add the eggs and vanillla extract. Mix until the batter is pale and fluffy.
  3. Sift in flour, salt and cocoa powder. Mix until combined.
  4. Add in the chocolate chips and fold into the batter until just combined.
  5. Pour the batter into a tin lined with baking sheet.
  6. Bake at 180 degrees celcius for 25 minutes.
  7. Let it cool completely and enjoy your fudgy brownie!
