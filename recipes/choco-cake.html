Tiny Chocolate Cake

Itty bitty choccy cake. Perfect for a tiny treat. Recipe by BakeLikeAPro.

Ingredients

  • Egg 1 medium one
  • Milk 3 tbsp
  • Granulated sugar 4 tbsp
  • Vegetable oil 3 tbsp
  • Salt 1/4 tsp
  • Cocoa powder 3 tbsp
  • Flour 4 tbsp
  • Baking powder 1 tsp

Instructions

  1. Whisk the egg and milk in a bowl.
  2. Add the sugar, vegetable oil and salt. Mix until combined.
  3. Sift in flour, baking powder and cocoa powder. Mix until combined.
  4. Pour the batter into an itty bitty cake tin lined with baking sheet.
  5. Bake at 180 degrees celcius for 25 minutes.
  6. Let it cool completely and enjoy your itty bitty treat!
